The pizza that we know and love today, with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese, originated in Naples, Italy,
in the late 18th century.
At that time, Naples was a bustling port city with a thriving working-class population.
Legend has it that the first pizza was created by a baker named Raffaele Esposito, who was asked
by the queen of Italy to make a pizza for her
visit to Naples.Esposito made a pizza with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese,
and basil to represent the colors of the Italian flag, and it became an instant hit.
